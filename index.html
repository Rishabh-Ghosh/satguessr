SatGuessr

Can you identify the world's cities purely from their satellite shapes?

How to Play

  • 🌍 You are placed at a random city's satellite view.
  • 🔍 Pan and zoom the satellite map to spot landmarks, rivers, coastlines, or street patterns.
  • 📍 Place a pin on the small world map in the corner to guess where you are.
  • ⏱️ You have 45 seconds per round!
  • 🔥 There are 5 rounds. The 5th (final) round counts for double points!
